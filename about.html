About Us

Founded in 2024 by Kenneth and Shadrack, IWB has grown to become a leader in electronic recycling and data recovery in Southern Africa. Our mission is to reduce e-waste and provide sustainable solutions for businesses and individuals.

Our Vision

To create a world where electronic waste is minimized, and valuable resources are reused responsibly.

Our Mission

To provide innovative and sustainable solutions for electronic recycling and data recovery, while educating the public on the importance of reducing e-waste.
